About PPPC

The Pan-Pacific PyLadies Conference brings together women interested in the Python open-source community. We aim to foster technical development and camaraderie between women from across the pacific region. For our first PPPC we will focus on PyLadies in the United States and Mexico, as entrepreneurs, developers and Python open-source contributors.
